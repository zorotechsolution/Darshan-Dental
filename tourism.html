Scenic Attractions in Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari is located at the southernmost tip of India, where the Arabian Sea, the Bay of Bengal, and the Indian Ocean meet. It is filled with varied and interesting tourist attractions.

01

Iconic Memorials & Statues

Visit the majestic Vivekananda Rock Memorial and the 133-foot Thiruvalluvar Statue standing in the sea, the Gandhi Memorial Mandapam, and the Kamarajar Mani Mantapa monument.

02

Historic Heritage & Forts

Explore the historic Bhagavathy Amman Temple, the ancient Padmanabhapuram Wooden Palace (an architectural wonder), and the scenic Vattakottai seaside fort.

03

Nature & Waterfalls

Take in the natural beauty of Muttom Beach with its famous lighthouse, Pechiparai Dam, Tirparappu Waterfalls, the therapeutic Maruthuvamalai hills, and Mukkadal reservoir.

04

Sunrise & Sunset Wonders

Kanyakumari is world-famous for its spectacular sunrise and sunset. On full-moon evenings (Chitra Pournami), you can witness the moon-rise and sunset simultaneously over the ocean.
