Areas of Special Interest

Dr. Tania brings specialised academic and clinical mastery in three key domains of modern orthodontics.

01

Orthodontics

Full spectrum of solutions — metal braces, ceramic braces, lingual braces, and Invisalign clear aligners. Tailored to each patient's anatomy with 13+ years of gold-medal expertise.

02

Micro Implant Anchorage

TADs — tiny titanium screws that provide a stable anchor point for complex tooth movements, achieving superior outcomes without surgery in many cases.

03

Skeletal Malocclusion

Jaw discrepancies like underbites, overbites and crossbites managed using CBCT 3D imaging, cephalometric analysis, and OPG for precision diagnosis and treatment.
