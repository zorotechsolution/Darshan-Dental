Areas of Special Interest

Dr. Tania brings specialised academic and clinical mastery in three key domains of modern orthodontics.

Speciality 01

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is the diagnosis, prevention, and correction of malpositioned teeth and jaws. Dr. Tania offers a full spectrum of orthodontic solutions — from traditional metal braces and ceramic braces to lingual braces and Invisalign clear aligners — tailored individually to each patient's anatomy and lifestyle.

Her gold-medal academic background and 13+ years of practice make her one of the most sought-after orthodontists in Kanyakumari and Nagercoil.

Speciality 02

Micro Implant Anchorage in Orthodontics

Temporary Anchorage Devices (TADs), or micro-implants, are tiny titanium screws placed in the jawbone to provide an absolute and stable anchor point during orthodontic tooth movement. This allows complex tooth movements that were previously impossible with conventional braces alone.

Dr. Tania's expertise in TAD-supported treatment means patients achieve superior outcomes — including vertical control, space closure and bite correction — without the need for surgery in many cases.

Speciality 03

Management of Skeletal Malocclusion

Skeletal malocclusion refers to jaw discrepancies — such as an underbite, overbite, or crossbite — caused by abnormal bone development rather than tooth position alone. These cases require carefully planned, multi-disciplinary management.

Dr. Tania employs advanced diagnostic tools including CBCT 3D imaging, cephalometric analysis, and OPG to precisely assess jaw relationships. Treatment may involve functional appliances during growth, orthodontic camouflage, or coordinated surgical-orthodontic planning for adult patients — delivering harmonious facial aesthetics and optimal function.
