Meet Our World-Class Dental Specialists

Dr. Millingtania, MDS Founder & Chief Orthodontist
Founder Profile

Dr. Millingtania, MDS

Founder & Chief Orthodontist, Darshan Dental Clinic

“Head of the Department – Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics, Rajas Dental College, Tirunelveli. Over 16 years of expertise in advanced orthodontics, Invisalign, and aesthetic smile rehabilitation.”

Dr. Millingtania, MDS is the Founder & Chief Orthodontist of Darshan Dental Clinic, a leading centre for advanced orthodontic and dental care in Kanyakumari District and Nagercoil. With over 16 years of experience in orthodontics and comprehensive dental care, she has established herself as one of the most trusted orthodontic specialists in the region. She also serves as the Head of the Department of Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics at Rajas Dental College, Tirunelveli, where she is actively involved in teaching, research, and mentoring future dental professionals.

She completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics from The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University under the merit quota. A consistent academic achiever, she was a topper and gold medallist throughout her educational journey.

Dr. Millingtania is widely recognized as a leading Invisalign and clear aligner specialist in Kanyakumari District and Nagercoil. As an Invisalign-certified provider, she has successfully managed a wide range of orthodontic cases using advanced digital treatment planning and customized clear aligner therapy. Her expertise in aesthetic orthodontics allows patients to achieve beautifully aligned smiles through comfortable, discreet, and predictable treatment options.

Driven by a vision to make advanced aligner treatment more accessible, she founded Darsh Aligners, the in-house clear aligner division of Darshan Dental Clinic. Combining digital smile analysis, advanced treatment planning, and customized aligner technology, Darsh Aligners provides precise, comfortable, and affordable orthodontic solutions. Today, Darshan Dental Clinic has become a preferred destination for Invisalign treatment and clear aligners in Kanyakumari District and Nagercoil, serving patients from across Tamil Nadu, India, and overseas.

In addition to orthodontics, Dr. Millingtania has expertise in implant dentistry and multidisciplinary smile rehabilitation, integrating orthodontics, implants, smile design, and restorative dentistry to achieve optimal functional and aesthetic outcomes.

An accomplished academician, researcher, and speaker, Dr. Millingtania has authored several scientific publications and has delivered lectures and scientific presentations at numerous national and international dental conferences. She has also mentored and trained several undergraduate and postgraduate dental students, contributing significantly to the advancement of orthodontic education and clinical practice.

Under her leadership, Darshan Dental Clinic has established itself as one of the most trusted centres for Invisalign, clear aligners, braces, smile design, and advanced orthodontic treatment in Kanyakumari District and Nagercoil. Her commitment to clinical excellence, innovation, research, and patient-centred care continues to make her a preferred choice for patients seeking modern orthodontic solutions and confident, healthy smiles.
